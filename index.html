*Simple spongy Cake*

:Ingrediants:

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 85g self-raising flour

: Name on ingredients :

Caster sugar
-finely granulated white sugar
self-raising flour
A pre-mixed combination of flour and leaving agents (usually salt and baking powder)

:Method:

  1. Preheat the ovan to 190C.
  2. Grease a 20Cm round cake pam.
  3. In a medium bowl, whip togather the eggs and caster sugar until fluffy.
  4. Fold in flour.
  5. pour misture into the prepare pan.
  6. Bake for 20minutes in the preheated ovan, or until the top of the cake springs back when lightly pressed.
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
